How a reading gets built

Element

Fire, Earth, Air or Water sets the temperament — how a sign moves through its day.

Modality

Cardinal, Fixed or Mutable describes whether a sign starts things, sustains them, or adapts them.

Ruler

The governing planet — Mars, Venus, Saturn and so on — points to where a sign's attention naturally falls.

Date

The calendar day combines with the above to produce that day's specific reading — consistent if you check back.
